# Portfolio Skill

## Purpose

Use this skill to organize a learner or project portfolio that shows growth, evidence, reflection, revision, and human validation.

## Portfolio principle

A portfolio is a learning story, not a trophy shelf.

## Minimum portfolio structure

```text
Goal
Context
Artifacts
Evidence
Reflection
Feedback received
Revision
Human validation
Next step
```

## Portfolio rules

- Keep learner voice visible.
- Make AI collaboration transparent when material.
- Link claims to artifacts or evidence.
- Include growth over time, not only polished final work.
- Separate private evidence from public summaries.
- Do not include private student information in public portfolios.

## Portfolio curator agent role

An AI portfolio curator may:
- organize artifacts;
- draft summaries;
- suggest missing evidence;
- create timelines;
- propose tags.

It may not:
- invent accomplishments;
- make claims beyond evidence;
- erase learner voice;
- issue final validation.

## Suggested portfolio sections

1. What I explored
2. What I built
3. What evidence I collected
4. What changed in my thinking
5. How I used AI responsibly
6. What a teacher or community reviewer validated
7. What I will try next
