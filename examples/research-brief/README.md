# Example: AI-Assisted Evidence Review Research Brief

**Status:** Synthetic demonstration  
**Related layer:** Brain  
**Human approval required:** Yes

## Question

Can an AI system help a teacher identify rubric-aligned evidence in a synthetic project reflection without making the final judgment?

## Proposed method

1. Provide a synthetic reflection and rubric.
2. Ask the AI to identify evidence and missing evidence.
3. Require confidence and limitations.
4. Have a human reviewer compare the recommendation against the rubric.
5. Document errors and improvements.

## Important limitation

This example is a workflow sketch. It does not demonstrate educational effectiveness or validate an AI system.
