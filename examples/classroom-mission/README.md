# Example: Environmental Sensor Mission

**Status:** Synthetic demonstration  
**Related layer:** Learning  
**Human approval required:** Yes

## Driving question

How can we use simple environmental data to propose one improvement to our classroom or school environment?

## Learner goal

Collect a small set of sensor observations, organize the data, explain one pattern, and propose an evidence-based improvement.

## Artifact

- A one-page data story.
- A simple chart or table.
- A reflection explaining choices and revisions.

## Evidence

- Synthetic sensor observations.
- Learner explanation.
- Teacher feedback note.
- Revision note.

## AI role

AI may help learners:
- brainstorm questions;
- organize a chart;
- ask reflection questions;
- explain vocabulary at an age-appropriate level.

AI may not:
- invent data;
- write the final reflection without learner input;
- issue a grade or badge.

## Human review

Teacher checks:
- data is plausible;
- claim matches evidence;
- learner can explain decisions;
- AI use is transparent;
- reflection shows revision.

## Stop condition

A teacher has reviewed the artifact and the learner has completed a reflection.
