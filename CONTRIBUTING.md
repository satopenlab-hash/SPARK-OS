# Contributing to OpenLab OS

OpenLab OS is in a private Genesis phase. Contributions are invited through careful, evidence-based improvement—not feature accumulation.

## Before proposing a change

Answer:

1. Which OpenLab layer does this affect?
2. What human problem does it solve?
3. What evidence suggests the need is real?
4. What could go wrong?
5. Who retains final authority?
6. Does this introduce student-data, privacy, or equity risk?
7. What is the smallest testable version?

## Contribution rules

- Keep artifacts portable and human-readable.
- Do not upload real student data.
- Do not add claims of effectiveness without evidence.
- Do not remove human review from learning or recognition workflows.
- Add a decision record for major architecture or governance changes.
- Prefer one narrow, testable improvement over a broad speculative feature.

## Commit style

Use plain, descriptive commit messages:

```text
Add teacher-validation template
Clarify evidence-level definitions
Document AI confidence requirements
Create synthetic sensor-mission example
```
